Mechatronics Project

This project is currently underway, and will take place from January to April 2016. We are tasked with configuring and programming a 2WD robot to accomplish autonomous navigation on a playfield, and to compete against other robots in a head-to-head goal-scoring competition. The Arduino board contains an ATMEGA328 microcontroller chip produced by ATMEL.
